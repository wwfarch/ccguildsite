Rules

We don't have many rules but we do enforce them strictly. If you are caught breaking a rule you will likely be kicked out of the guild.

  1. Be courteous. Everyone in the guild is reasonable so there's no need to be rude.
  2. Read and understand the schedule. The guild can only succeed if people are online for guild events.
  3. Do not do anything the harm the guild. This includes not following directions during guild events like torch battles.
